About QuackuAni

Meet the Owner

QuackuAni was created by Sophia Ramos, also known as “Chin,” a second-year college student from National University Mall of Asia. As someone who enjoys cute visuals, cozy designs, and anime movies or series, she built this website as a fun space for anime fans who want easier and more aesthetic recommendations.

Why “QuackuAni”?

The name QuackuAni combines “quack,” inspired by the cute duck theme of the website, and “ani,” which stands for anime. Together, it creates a playful and memorable name that reflects the site’s soft duck-inspired design while still focusing on anime recommendations, moods, genres, and shows worth discovering.

Explore Anime

QuackuAni lets users browse different anime movies and series based on genres, popularity, and personal mood. Each anime card leads to a detailed page with its title, director, writer, synopsis, release date, running time, popular quotes, and related recommendations, making it easier to decide what to watch next.
